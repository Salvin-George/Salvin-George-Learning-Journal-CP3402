## Learning Activities & Resources

- This week I focused on learning how to set up and use team collaboration tools for a web development project, including GitHub for version control, Trello for project planning, and Discord for communication.

- I also reviewed how project management tools like Trello can be used to organise tasks, assign responsibilities, and track progress across a team.

## Resources used:

- https://trello.com/guide

## Estimated Hours

- About 4 hours (spent 2 hours after session getting familar with trello)

## Content Insights

- Using Trello helped me understand how project planning is structured in a team environment. Breaking the project into tasks and assigning responsibilities makes the workflow clearer and helps ensure accountability among team members.

- I also learned the importance of integrating tools (such as linking GitHub with communication platforms), as this improves team awareness and keeps everyone updated on progress.

## Career/Employability/Learning Insights


- From a learning perspective, I realised that collaboration introduces a different type of responsibility compared to individual work. Being accountable to a team encourages more consistent participation and better organisation. This week also gave me a full grasp on how important it was to assign tasks and ensure that team members were on track and progressing.
